# Finished the electronics plate

### Importing parts

To make the electronics plate, I needed a model of all the parts so that I could add them to my sketch. 

I found the CAD files for these parts on GrabCAD. I imported the step files into my Onshape document.

### Initial Sketch 

After that, I arranged all the parts in the orientation that I wanted them to be in the final transmitter. Where the joysticks are 50 mm apart, the radio is in the upper left corner, etc. 

Then I used the edit in context feature to be able to see all the parts from the assembly in the parts studio. 

I then made a sketch and used the USE tool in order to add the mounting holes and other features to my sketch. 

After that, I sketched the outline of my controller. Use measured an IR controller, copied most of its dimensions.
