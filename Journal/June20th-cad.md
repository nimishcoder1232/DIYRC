## Making the receiver 

I started by placing the two components in the orientation I want them for the final design. 

After that, I did an edit in context again, made a sketch, and made mounting holes for the bottom plate. I used the edit in context in order to avoid placing a hole through a part. 
I also made a hole for the radio wires.

I landed on this:
<img width="982" height="672" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7e1699f3-e311-4999-9cf7-b6c1c50e8dac" />

After that, I extruded the face again to make a new part, then I shelled it to make space inside. Then I made a hole on the side for the usb. Then I filleted it.
